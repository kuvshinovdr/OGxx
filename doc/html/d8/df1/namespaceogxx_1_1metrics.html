auto ogxx::metrics::new_p_norm_metrics ( Float  p) -> Metrics_uptr

Computes the sum of absolute differences raised to power p and then raises it to 1/p power. Special cases: p = 0 -> Hamming, p = 1 -> Manhattan, p = 2 -> Euclidian, p = inf -> Chebyshev.
